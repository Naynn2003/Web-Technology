LD College of Engineering, Ahmedabad

About LD College of Engineering

LD College of Engineering is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering colleges in Gujarat, India. It was established in 1948 and is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines of engineering.
